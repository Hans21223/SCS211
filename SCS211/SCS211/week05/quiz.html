Gründer Industries, officially North Osea Gründer Industries or
Osea Gründer Industry Group (I.G.) and formerly

The ADF-11F Raven is a seventh-generation fighter aircraft and the
latest in the Advanced Dominance Fighter series. The Raven uniquely
combines separate nose and wing units to create one aircraft. Using data
from an onboard AI, the Raven dominates in close-range dogfights.

human pilot or a detachable UAV. In manned units, the COFFIN data is sent to a
helmet-mounted display.
