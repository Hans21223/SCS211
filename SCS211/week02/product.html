Development

Development of the CFA-44 began on June 1, 2005, in Estovakia. It began
development as a carrier-based fighter under a national project involving several
weapons manufacturing firms. Central to the project was the state-run
Albastru-Electrice, who aimed to create an aerial arsenal ship capable of
seamless datalink with autonomous Malebolge UAV-45s. Eventually, a
prototype was developed, with its main focus being the successful integration of
an Electromagnetic Launcher on a fighter.
